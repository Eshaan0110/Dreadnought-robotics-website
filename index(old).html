Who We Are
ABOUT
THE TEAM

Dreadnought Robotics is a student-led autonomous systems team at VIT Chennai focused on building AUVs for international competitions.

Our interdisciplinary crew brings together robotics, AI, computer vision, embedded systems, and mechanical design. We operate at the intersection of research and engineering.

From concept to competition pool — every AUV represents hundreds of hours of design, simulation, testing, and iteration.

Singapore Autonomous Underwater Vehicle Challenge
Location
Singapore · Annual International Competition
About
Asia's most prestigious AUV competition. Teams from universities globally must complete all missions fully autonomously — zero human intervention during runs.
Competition Tasks
  • Gate Navigation
  • Buoy Targeting & Touching
  • Torpedo Firing
  • Object Dropping into Bins
  • Acoustic Homing
  • Position Hold
