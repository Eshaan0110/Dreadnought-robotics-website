Who We Are
ABOUT
THE TEAM

Dreadnought Robotics is a student-led autonomous systems team at VIT Chennai focused on building AUVs for international competitions.

Our interdisciplinary crew brings together robotics, AI, computer vision, embedded systems, and mechanical design.

From concept to competition pool — every AUV represents hundreds of hours of design, simulation, testing, and iteration.

Robotics Computer Vision Embedded Systems Mechanical Design AI / Deep Learning Control Systems
